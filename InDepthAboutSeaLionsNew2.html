In Depth With Sea Lions

What do Sea Lions Eat?

Fish

Sea Lions eat lots of fish to maintain a healthy diet.

Where do Sea Lions Live?

Sea lions live in various places across the world, but typically staying around colder bodies of water.

Sea Lions are typically known to hunt together in packs

Sea lions seem to know when it is good to hunt together,

They usually do so when prey is abundant. They feed on more than 50 species of fishes and cephalopods, feeding primarily on squids, octopuses, hake, northern anchovy, opaleye, and herring.

Are they the Hunters or the Hunted?

Although it may seem as if Sea Lions have it easy,

They are preyed upon by orcas and sharks, as well as coyotes, feral dogs and bald eagles hunting for pups while on land.

Disease, parasites and the weather system El Nino also take their toll. They are also vulnerable to the effects of climate change on ocean currents, which impacts their fish prey abundance.
